────── What is Green Scouts? ──────

Green Scouts is a recycling companion app that provides useful information about what you can recycle, where you can recycle and other interesting facts about recycling. You are able to document your recycling history and earn fun badges when completing certain milestones. We hope to motivate people to recycle consistently as well as keep people informed.
